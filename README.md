# RentReady Planner

A modern MVP for a first-apartment cost and rent affordability planner.

## What it does

- Calculates recommended rent range from income, debts, transportation, utilities, and other monthly obligations.
- Estimates move-in cash needed: first month, deposit, fees, moving, furniture, utility deposits, and emergency cushion.
- Labels the lease as comfortable, tight, or risky.
- Compares three apartment options by monthly and first-year cost.
- Generates a personalized pre-signing checklist.
- Includes an MVP email capture placeholder for a future mailing-list or PDF flow.

## Why this MVP

The product targets renter intent rather than a vague age demographic. The useful question is not “what do young adults need?” It is “can I afford this apartment and what cash do I need before I sign?” That search intent is clearer, more monetizable, and easier to defend with SEO content.
